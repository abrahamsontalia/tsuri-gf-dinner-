Gluten-Free Sushi Dinner at Tsuri

Join Beyond Celiac Ambassadors Talia Abrahamson and Hayden Bishop for a special set-course dinner at Tsuri featuring its curated selection of celiac-safe, gluten-free Japanese dishes, available almost nowhere else. Thanks to Tsuri's dedicated gluten-free fryer, the menu spotlights crispy rice, tempura, specialty rolls and house-made gluten-free sauces. It is a delicious evening where the Los Angeles gluten-free community can finally relax and enjoy a safe, thoughtfully prepared meal together.

Wednesday, July 1 6:00 PM Tsuri • Melrose

About this event

Join us for a special gluten-free dinner at Tsuri, featuring a menu designed to showcase the restaurant's incredible gluten-free offerings. From crispy rice and tempura to ponzu and fried ice cream, this meal highlights the creativity and care that make Tsuri a favorite among the gluten-free community.

Why Tsuri?
Tsuri has a dedicated gluten-free fryer and follows strict protocols to help prevent cross-contact in the kitchen.
